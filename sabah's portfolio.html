Who I Am

About Me

I'm a 1st year AI & Data Science student who got into tech because I was obsessed with how things look and feel — not just how they work.

My thing is vibe coding — building with energy and intention, where the design and the code feel like they belong together. I care deeply about UI/UX and think most apps are ugly and needlessly confusing.

I'm learning React, Node.js and databases by building real things that real people use. QueueCare is already live in production and won 2nd prize at a buildathon — and that's just the beginning.

Long term? Build tech that solves real problems for underserved communities — and make it beautiful while doing it.
